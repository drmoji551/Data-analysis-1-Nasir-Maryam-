## Project Overview
This project presents the analysis of sales transaction records spanning 2023 to 2025. it includes information on product sold,quantity sold, total revenue, payment methods, order status, transaction dates, coupon codes and referral sources.
The goal was to uncover trends, identify outliers, and generate actionable business insights.

## Methodology
| Step | Tool | Description |
|------|------|-------------|

| Exploratory Analysis | Excel & PostgreSQL | Calculated KPIs and investigated patterns and outliers |

| Reporting | PowerPoint | Created an executive summary with key findings and recommendations |

## Key Insights
- Sales declining: 25.7% drop from 2023 to 2025 from 43.7% to 18.3% of total sales which could be due to lack of repeat customer strategy
- Sales performance varied considerably across months. January recorded the highest monthly revenue at ₹124,313.23, while September recorded the lowest at ₹69,321.65, this could be because of reduced promotion activity in September. 
- Chair leads revenue: Chair accounts for 15.4% of total revenue (₹1,95,620.11). phones recorded the lowest sales figures among all product categories. Phone's low revenue despite reasonable order volume suggests low unit prices or heavy discounting
- In terms of order volume, tablets were the most frequently ordered product, with 179 orders placed during the period.
- High cancellation: A total of 231 orders were successfully delivered; however, 250 orders were cancelled which could be due to product-description mismatches, poor quality control, or slow delivery times frustrating customers.
- Cart Abandonment The total number of items added to carts stood at 6,582, of which 3,535 items were actually sold, reflecting an overall cart-to-sale conversion rate of approximately 53.7%. Likely causes include unexpected shipping costs at checkout, a complicated checkout process, or customers browsing without intent to buy immediately.
- Instagram was the leading referral source, generating 259 visits. The most widely used coupon code was FREESHIP, used 313 times, indicating strong customer responsiveness to free shipping incentives. No single promotion and referral source is outperforming significantly.
- Online payment was the most preferred payment method, recorded in 258 transactions, while gift cards represented the least utilized payment option during the review period.
## Recommendations
- Rekindle customer relationships through personalised email campaigns.
- Improve product descriptions and images to set accurate expectations.
- Follow up on the 237 pending and 235 shipped orders that have no resolution.
- Mid-year clearance sale, flash sales or discounts can be done to boost the third and fourth quarter.
- Phone pricing strategy should be reviewed, it may be underpriced relative to demand.
- Promote tablets (highest order count at 179) more aggressively since customers already favour them.
- Set up automated cart abandonment reminders via email within 1–24 hours.
- Display shipping costs upfront before checkout to avoid surprises.
- Gift card campaign can be launched during festive or gifting seasons with a bonus credit incentive.
